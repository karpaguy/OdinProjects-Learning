Generic Food

This is a very generic food. You will like it.

Ingredients

  • 10g Pepper
  • 1 Can (200g) of Beans
  • 1 Bread
  • A bunch of vegetables.

Steps

  1. Cut the pepper and all the vegetables in a bowl.
  2. Cut the bread open and left it in a plate.
  3. Open your can of beans.
  4. Enjoy it!
